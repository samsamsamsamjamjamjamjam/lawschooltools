PowerPoint Generator

Paste your notes (or upload a .txt file). We'll turn them into a clean academic PowerPoint — one concept per slide. Add a printable handout if you want one.

How to use this (click to open)
  1. Paste your notes in the box below, or click Choose File to upload a .txt file.
  2. Check Include handout if you also want a one-page summary you can print.
  3. Click Generate PowerPoint. Your computer will download a .pptx file you can open in PowerPoint, Google Slides, or Keynote.

Quiz Me

Drop in your notes and get random practice questions — one at a time. Great for retention. Choose fill-in-the-blank or multiple choice before you start.

How to use this (click to open)
  1. Paste your notes (or upload a .txt file).
  2. Pick a question style: fill in the blank or multiple choice.
  3. Click Start Quizzing. A random question appears.
  4. Think about your answer, then click Show Answer. Click Next Question for another.
